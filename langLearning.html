This page is a collection of useful language learning resources geared towards helping immigrants relearn and reconnect with their language.

Tandem, a free real-time chat app to chat with anybody online, voice-to-voice and via text. Simply find a partner and get started chatting to fluency.

Duolingo, THE language learning app. Duolingo features practice exercises, speaking practice and stories to help with reading comprehension and response writing.

HelloTalk focusses on real-time communcation between users. HelloTalk acts as a social media platform based on language learners.

Anki, a flashcard app with many technical features to help you memorize information clearly and concisely.
